Detailed Project Description (no more than 3-4 sentences)

Our team loves burner wallet for its usability and xDai’s fast transaction. But there is a lack of privacy, each vendor whom I pay can see the history of my transactions. Luckily we can solve this issue with zkSNARKs!

We are bringing privacy to burner wallet (all fincitonality impemented directly in Burner wallet fork and DAI infrastructure with confidential micropayments. :sunglasses:

Usage:

  • open Burner wallet fork by link: zdai.io :iphone:
  • Exchange xDAI to zDAI :currency_exchange:
  • Send confidetial private transaction :arrow_heading_up:
  • ... ( take a cup of ☕️, while zkSNARK proof calculating approx 1 minute)
  • Profit! :tada:
